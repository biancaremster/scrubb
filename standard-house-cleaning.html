The Standard Cleaning Checklist

Our standard house cleaning covers all the essential recurring tasks required to maintain a tidy and healthy home. We recommend this clean for clients wanting recurring cleans, and we offer discounted rates for recurring weekly and bi-weekly schedules. Here is exactly what is included in every visit, room by room.

Bathroom

We sanitize and shine every fixture, ensuring your bathroom stays fresh and hygienic.

  • Remove items from floor
  • Dust all surfaces
  • Clean & sanitize toilet
  • Clean & scrub shower / tub
  • Polish mirror
  • Wipe down countertops
  • Wipe down doorknobs & light switches
  • Wipe down exterior cabinets
  • Clean & sanitize sink
  • Sweep & mop floor
  • Empty trash & replace bag
  • Wipe exterior of trash can

Kitchen

We clean grease, dust, and crumbs from countertops, sink, and exterior appliances.

  • Dust reachable surfaces
  • Wipe exterior vent hood
  • Wipe exterior dishwasher
  • Wipe exterior refrigerator
  • Wipe exterior oven & cooktops
  • Wipe exterior microwave
  • Wipe counters & small appliances
  • Wipe exterior cabinets
  • Disinfect doorknobs & light switches
  • Spot check surfaces
  • Clean & sanitize sink
  • Empty trash & replace bag
  • Wipe exterior of trash can
  • Sweep & mop floor

Dining Room

A tidy dining space for sharing meals, free from dust and fingerprints.

  • Dust all surfaces
  • Clean mirrors & glass surfaces
  • Light dust furniture (excl. upholstery)
  • Wipe doorknobs & light switches
  • Spot check for marks
  • Vacuum or sweep & mop floor

Living Room

We dust and clean your central gathering space, keeping it welcoming and cozy.

  • Dust all reachable areas
  • Clean mirrors & glass frames
  • Dust furniture surfaces
  • Wipe doorknobs & light switches
  • Spot check general surfaces
  • Vacuum carpets & sweep/mop hard floors

Bedrooms

A fresh, dust-free sleeping environment to help you rest easy every night.

  • Dust dresser, nightstands, and headboard
  • Clean mirrors & glass
  • Light dust bedroom furniture
  • Wipe doorknobs & light switches
  • Vacuum carpet & sweep/mop floors

Hallways & Foyer

First impressions count. We keep your home's transition spaces clean and bright.

  • Dust reachable ledges & railings
  • Clean foyer mirrors & glass
  • Wipe doorknobs & light switches
  • Dust any entryway furniture
  • Spot check doors & frames
  • Vacuum or sweep & mop floor
